Below are all the 2e **classes** from the official 2e rulebooks.

Also check out [Classes (Dragon Magazine)](/docs/classes_(dragon_magazine)).

## Core Classes

![Bard](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/adnd2e/images/8/82/Bard.png/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/180?cb=20230205121904)

### [Bard](/docs/bard)

(Rogue Group)

_Bards make their way in life by their charm, talent, and wit. A good bard should be glib of tongue, light of heart, and fleet of foot (when all else fails)._

![Cleric](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/adnd2e/images/8/88/Cleric.png/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/180?cb=20230205121904)

### [Cleric](/docs/cleric)

(Priest Group)

_The Cleric is a generic priest (of any mythos) who tends to the needs of a community. They are both protector and healer. They are not purely defensive, however. When evil threatens, the cleric is well-suited to seek it out on its own ground and destroy it._

-   [Cleric Class from Player's Option: Spells & Magic](/docs/cleric_(posm))

![Druid](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/adnd2e/images/9/9d/Druid.png/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/180?cb=20230205122727)

### [Druid](/docs/druid)

(Priest Group)

_The druid serves the cause of nature and neutrality; the wilderness is their community. They use their special powers to protect it and to preserve balance in the world._

-   [Druid Class from Player's Option: Spells & Magic](/docs/druid_(posm))

![Fighter](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/adnd2e/images/e/e6/Fighter.png/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/180?cb=20230205121905)

### [Fighter](/docs/fighter)

(Warrior Group)

_The fighter is a champion, swordsman, soldier, and brawler. They live or die by their knowledge of weapons and tactics. Fighters can be found at the front of any battle, contesting toe-to-toe with monsters and villains. A good fighter needs to be strong and healthy if they hope to survive._

![Wizard](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/adnd2e/images/c/c7/Wizard.png/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/180?cb=20230205122922)

### [Mage](/docs/mage)

(Wizard Group)

_Mages are the most versatile types of wizards, those who choose not to specialize in any single school of magic._

![Paladin](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/adnd2e/images/b/b2/Paladin.png/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/180?cb=20230205121904)

### [Paladin](/docs/paladin)

(Warrior Group)

_The paladin is a warrior bold and pure, the exemplar of everything good and true. Like the fighter, the paladin is a person of combat. However, the paladin lives for the ideals of righteousness, justice, honesty, piety, and chivalry. They strive to be a living example of these virtues so that others might learn from them as well as gain by their actions._

![Ranger](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/adnd2e/images/e/e9/Ranger.png/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/180?cb=20230205121904)

### [Ranger](/docs/ranger)

(Warrior Group)

_The ranger is a warrior and a woodsman. They are skilled with weapons and is knowledgeable in tracking and woodcraft. The ranger often protects and guides lost travelers and honest peasant-folk. A ranger needs to be strong and wise to the ways of nature to live a full life._

![Sorcerer](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/adnd2e/images/2/27/Sorcerer.png/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/180?cb=20230205121904)

(Rogue Group)

_The profession of thief is not honorable, yet it is not entirely dishonorable, either. The thief is a romantic hero fired by noble purpose but a little wanting in strength of character. Such a person may truly strive for good but continually run afoul of temptation._

## Setting Specific Classes

**Dark Sun**

![Gladiator](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/adnd2e/images/4/44/Gladiator.png/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/180?cb=20230404011346)

### [Gladiator](/docs/gladiator)

(Warrior Group) - _Dark Sun Campaign Setting Revised_

_Gladiators are the slave warriors of the city states, specially trained for brutal physical contests. Disciplined in many diverse forms of hand-to-hand combat and skilled in the use of dozens of different weapons, gladiators are the most dangerous warriors on Athas._

![Defiler](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/adnd2e/images/4/41/Defiler.png/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/180?cb=20230404113916)

### [Defiler](/docs/defiler)

(Wizard Group) - _Dark Sun Campaign Setting Revised_

_Defilers are wizards who have decided to take a faster, darker approach to mastering the use of magical spells. In the give and take of spell casting, defilers are well versed in the taking, but give nothing in return. With every spell cast, a defiler leeches the life-energy out of the plants and soil around him, leaving a lifeless zone._

![Preserver](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/adnd2e/images/5/5f/Preserver.png/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/180?cb=20230404113834)

### [Preserver](/docs/preserver)

(Wizard Group) - _Dark Sun Campaign Setting Revised_

The preserver is a [wizard](/docs/wizard) who seeks to maintain the environment while drawing magical power from it. In the give and take of spell casting, preservers have mastered the balance. A preservers magical spells are cast in harmony with nature, and there is no damage to the sur rounding environment.

![Templar](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/adnd2e/images/0/0e/Templar.png/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/180?cb=20230404140714)

### [Templar](/docs/templar)

(Priest Group) - _Dark Sun Campaign Setting Revised_

![Trader](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/adnd2e/images/0/06/Trader.png/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/180?cb=20230404034523)

### [Trader](/docs/trader)

(Rogue Group) - _Dark Sun Campaign Setting_

_The trader is a rogue who specializes in commerce and mercantile endeavors. Hagglers of great skill and procurers of fertile imagination, traders are welcome throughout Athas by ordinary citizens for the goods they deal in._
